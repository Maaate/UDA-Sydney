Proven Track Record

Our main goal at UDA is to improve your skills in self-defence, build confidence and encourage an overall healthy lifestyle through martial arts. While competition is not our main focus, we certainly have a strong history of being good at it.

We've trained Australia's first Abu Dhabi World Professional Jiu Jitsu Gi and No Gi Champions, Australia's first Kid's World Champions, and our students have won countless numbers of State and National Championships.
